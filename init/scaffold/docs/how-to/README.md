# How-to guides

Task-oriented documentation. A how-to guide answers a specific
"how do I X?" question for someone who already has the basics.

## What belongs here

- "How to deploy to staging."
- "How to rotate database credentials."
- "How to add a new feature flag."
- Goal-oriented; assumes the reader knows the lay of the land.

## What does NOT belong here

- First-contact walkthroughs → `../tutorials/`.
- Reference tables for every flag → `../reference/`.
- Why the system works this way → `../explanation/`.

## Style

- One guide per task. Title states the goal.
- Steps are minimal — only what the reader must do to achieve the
  goal; no explanations of why.
- Variations and edge cases handled inline; do not branch into
  half-tutorial territory.
